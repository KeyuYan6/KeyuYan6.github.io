    • Biography

    I have received the B.E. degree in electronic and information engineering from the Dalian University of Technology (DUT), Dalian, China, in 2021. I am currently a M.S. student at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), Hefei, China. My current research interests span computer vision and machine learning with a series of topics, such as image fusion, image generation, depth super-resolution, image restoration and object detection.
